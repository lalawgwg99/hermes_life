# Dream Cycle (minimal)

Goal: nightly maintenance that makes the brain smarter with zero manual work.

## Inputs
- New files in inbox/
- Recent commits in the brain repo

## Steps
1) Summarize new items and changes from the last 24h.
2) Extract entities (people/companies/ideas) mentioned in inbox items.
3) Propose file moves from inbox/ to the right folders.
4) Append a daily dream log in meta/dreams/YYYY-MM-DD.md.

## Safety
- Do NOT overwrite compiled truth without a source.
- Timeline is append-only.
- Human edits always win.
